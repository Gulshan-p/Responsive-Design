Get to know your dentist

We welcome questions from health-conscious patients and work to make sure you’re satisfied with the answers we provide. If you’ve seen another Chicago dentist recently and want a second opinion, we’re happy to help!

Our holistic Chicago dentist office offers convenient hours that let you fit a visit into your busy day, whether it’s before or after work or on your lunch break.

Complimentary session

At Choices in Dentistry, we offer 30-minute complimentary get-to-know-you sessions. You’ll talk with a holistic dentist about your health history and any trouble you’re having with your mouth.

You’ll also get a quick and non-invasive microscopic screening of your teeth and gums to assess their health. Your complimentary session can be conducted in person or via online televisit.

you come first

Choices in Dentistry is more than a name — it’s the philosophy we’ve been committed to for the 30+ years. You’re one of a kind and dentistry is never one-size-fits-all!

When you visit our Chicago Loop dentist office, that’s exactly what you’ll find: choices. You’ll get the information you need to make the best possible decision for your smile and health.
